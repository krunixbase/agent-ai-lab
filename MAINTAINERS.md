## Maintainer Responsibilities

- Review and merge pull requests  
- Ensure architectural consistency  
- Maintain documentation quality  
- Oversee safety, ethics, and governance compliance  
- Approve releases and versioning  
- Manage roadmap updates  
- Resolve disputes and technical disagreements  

---

## Becoming a Maintainer

New maintainers may be added based on:

- consistent high‑quality contributions  
- demonstrated understanding of the architecture  
- adherence to safety and governance principles  
- positive participation in discussions and reviews  

Approval requires:

- nomination by an existing maintainer  
- confirmation by the repository owner  

---

## Maintainer Removal

A maintainer may be removed if they:

- violate the Code of Conduct  
- repeatedly introduce unsafe or harmful changes  
- become inactive for extended periods  
- act against the project’s goals or governance  

Removal requires approval by the repository owner.
