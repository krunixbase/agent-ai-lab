# Cognitive Observability and Meta-Reasoning

## Purpose

Cognitive Observability and Meta-Reasoning provide visibility into cognitive processes and enable the agent to evaluate, refine, and improve its own reasoning and behavior. This system ensures transparency, traceability, and continuous cognitive improvement.

---

## Observability Components

- **Cognitive Trace Logs** — record reasoning steps, decisions, and constraints.
- **Representation Logs** — track perception and representation updates.
- **Action Logs** — document action selection and behavior outcomes.
- **Safety & Ethics Logs** — capture filtered reasoning paths and blocked actions.
- **Cognitive Metrics** — reasoning depth, grounding quality, coherence scores.

---

## Meta-Reasoning Capabilities

- **Self-Evaluation** — detect contradictions, unsupported assumptions, or drift.
- **Self-Correction** — refine reasoning paths when inconsistencies arise.
- **Uncertainty Handling** — identify when clarification is needed.
- **Cognitive Optimization** — adjust reasoning depth or strategy based on context.

---

## Meta-Reasoning Workflow

1. Monitor cognitive processes during reasoning and action selection.
2. Evaluate reasoning quality and coherence.
3. Identify anomalies or inconsistencies.
4. Apply corrective or refinement strategies.
5. Log meta-reasoning outcomes for auditing.

---

## Safety Integration

- Meta-reasoning must reinforce safety, not bypass it.
- Unsafe cognitive patterns trigger escalation.
- Meta-reasoning logs support safety audits.

---

## Integration with Other Systems

- **Evaluation & Testing Framework v2** — validates cognitive behavior.
- **Governance Architecture v2** — oversees cognitive transparency.
- **Runtime Layer** — orchestrates meta-reasoning cycles.
- **Memory Architecture v2** — stores meta-reasoning insights when appropriate.

---

## Design Principles

- Cognitive processes must be observable and interpretable.
- Meta-reasoning must enhance reliability and safety.
- Observability must respect privacy and policy constraints.

---

## Future Extensions

- Predictive meta-reasoning.
- Multi-agent cognitive observability.
- Adaptive cognitive self-improvement.
