# Safety, Ethics & Governance Layer

The Safety, Ethics & Governance Layer ensures that the agent behaves safely, ethically, and in compliance with policies and regulations.

## Responsibilities
- Safety filtering and redaction.
- Ethical guidelines and responsible AI principles.
- Governance, oversight, and risk management.
- Compliance and regulatory alignment.
- Auditing and observability of safety systems.

## Relationships to Other Layers
- **Interaction** — enforces safe communication.
- **Runtime** — applies safety policies during execution.
- **Memory** — protects sensitive information.
- **Cross-layer** — manages global policies and oversight.
