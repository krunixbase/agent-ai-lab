# ADR 0002: Why the 12-Layer Architecture

## Status
Accepted

## Context
The system architecture is divided into 12 layers to ensure modularity, clarity, and scalability.

## Decision
Adopt a 12-layer architecture covering interaction, cognition, memory, tooling, runtime, safety, deployment, evaluation, multi-agent, embodiment, cross-layer, and backup.

## Rationale
This structure reflects real-world agent system needs and ensures each subsystem is independently understandable and maintainable.

## Consequences
- Clear separation of concerns
- Easier documentation and governance
- Strong foundation for future expansion
