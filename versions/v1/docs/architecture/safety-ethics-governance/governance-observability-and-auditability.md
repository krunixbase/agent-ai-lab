# Governance Observability and Auditability

## Purpose

Governance Observability and Auditability provide visibility into governance decisions, policy enforcement, oversight actions, and risk management. This system ensures transparency, accountability, and long-term reliability of governance processes.

---

## Observability Components

- **Governance Logs** — policy decisions, overrides, and enforcement actions.
- **Oversight Logs** — human review events and interventions.
- **Risk Logs** — detected risks, classifications, and escalations.
- **Policy Change Logs** — configuration and policy updates.
- **Compliance Logs** — regulatory checks and violations.
- **Governance Metrics** — oversight frequency, escalation rates, policy adherence.

---

## Auditing Objectives

- Detect governance regressions or inconsistencies.
- Ensure policy enforcement is deterministic and aligned with expectations.
- Provide traceability for critical decisions and interventions.
- Support compliance audits and regulatory reporting.
- Enable data-driven improvements to governance policies.

---

## Auditing Pipeline

1. Collect governance-related events and metrics.
2. Classify events by subsystem and category.
3. Analyze patterns, anomalies, and regressions.
4. Generate structured audit reports.
5. Feed insights into governance, safety, and configuration improvements.

---

## Integration with Other Systems

- **Safety Architecture** — correlates safety and governance events.
- **Runtime Layer** — provides execution context for governance decisions.
- **Deployment Architecture v2** — supplies environment-level governance signals.
- **Configuration & Policy Layer v2** — validates policy enforcement.
- **Testing & Validation Framework v2** — ensures governance compliance.

---

## Design Principles

- Transparent and structured logging.
- Strong safety and compliance alignment.
- Deterministic and reproducible governance traces.
- Actionable insights for system evolution.

---

## Future Extensions

- Real-time governance dashboards.
- Automated detection of governance anomalies.
- Cross-layer governance correlation.
